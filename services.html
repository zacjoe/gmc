Organizational Development Consultancy

We specialize in analyzing the potential development areas within an organization before advancing any organization development strategies to address an organization’s human capital issues and concerns.

We enable organizations to achieve their organizational transformation objectives by developing organizational improvement strategies via consulting projects, process improvement, learning and development workshops.

Our experienced consultants, are dedicated to enhance organizational strategic priorities by analyzing, diagnosing, evaluating, designing, developing and delivering performance improvement solutions focusing on competency-based performance standards, employee development, change management and strategic planning

Career Development and Management Consultancy

We specialize in career development and career management consultation that bring about greater enjoyment and growth opportunities in employee careers.

We assist organizations to retain talented employees by helping them in their career pursuit, build their personal credibility and expand their individual employability across their organization.

We design and develop career development workshops to facilitate an organization’s workforce exploration of individual career aspiration, passion, personal values and interest in order to achieve clearer career mapping and career direction.

Talent Management Framework

We specialize in developing talent management frameworks focusing on recruitment and selection process, performance management system, employee development and reward system.

We provide structured manpower planning tools to assess human resource supply and demand of skilled workers and assist organizations to reach their desired manpower position.

We assist organizations to transform through effective performance management systems focusing on employee performance and potential index; we also provide appropriate succession planning tools to accelerate performance.

Learning Organization and Performance Improvement

We specialize in sustaining organizational competitiveness by increasing workforce learning agility through stimulating new learnings and creative processes to ensure swift transformations and continuous adaptation to an ever changing and challenging environment.

We support organizations to achieve Learning Organization Aspiration through team learning, expand individual self efficacy through cross-fertilization of ideas, develop a high performing culture that serves to achieve organizational transformation.

We provide organizations with performance improvement consulting through job analysis, competency-based performance standard development, performance metrics and indicator design and accelerate performance through 70-20-10 learning methodology,

Organizational Transformation and Change

We provide consulting and training in managing organizational transformation by accelerating the process of change so that organizations will accomplish significantly better results with more positive outcomes in a rapidly changing environment.

We specialize in reviewing organizational systems, strategies, people, processes, structures and people capabilities and supporting organizational transformation through effective change and organizational transition management.

Systems Thinking and Strategic Leadership Capabilities

Gain deep appreciation and understanding of mass inter-relationships which exist among the human activity systems that affect or influence the operation efficiency and strategic attainment.

Learn to strategize business transactions through integrated systems thinking approach that increases synchronization of multiple world views and mental models among human capital resources in driving business success.

Build the essence of systems thinking as part of strategic leadership capabilities that serves as mindset shift and a framework that is able to identify interrelationships (the bigger picture) rather than linear cause-effect chains, and see processes of change rather than the snapshots of transformation.

Systematic Innovation and In-sourcing Solution

We provide organizations with systematic innovation tools to solve and breakthrough problems by seeing the interrelation of issues arising from different systems and sub-systems in an organization.

We support organizational competitiveness through subconscious breakout of traditional thinking patterns by helping organizations learn about connectivity and relationship of issues as part of a whole system thus allowing organizations to reach innovative solutions.

We assist organizations to understand the connectivity and relationship of organizational issues, acquire the skill of reframing thinking patterns and become agile when dealing with complex and wide ranging issues.

Strategic Business Negotiation and Planning

We specialize in designing strategic business planning programs focusing on business situations from both: organization’s perspective and competitor’s perspective.

We deliver essential strategic decision techniques for business leaders to predict their competitors’ reactions toward business decisions and influence them to their advantage.
