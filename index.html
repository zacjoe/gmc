Welcome to Techtual Consulting.

Techtual Consulting provides specialized Organization Development (OD) Consultancy; We develop learning and development programs in the fields of behavioral management; our programs enhance employee performance and support organizational transformation. Techtual’s group of consultants work internationally and has vast experience across a broad range of business sectors and industries. We lead and deliver customized OD projects and programs to enhance an organization’s human capital capabilities.

Techtual Mission and Priorities

Leverages on OD Disciplines

Techtual is a leading provider that leverages on Organization Development (OD) disciplines to enable organizations to meet their strategic transformation and tactical goals by bringing forth a high performing culture and advancing their human capital capabilities from within.

Growth and Learning

It is Techtual’s business mission to bring growth and learning to workforces and develop talents that positively impact essential business process and the bottom line.

Customizes Projects

Techtual customizes consulting projects, learning programs and workshops based on the development culture of organizations; we build a sustainable process to support organization development
